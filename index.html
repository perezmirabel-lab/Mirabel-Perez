Professional Summary

I am a dedicated professional with over 12 years of experience in the U.S. Army (2000–2012), specializing in aviation systems, electrical troubleshooting, and team leadership. My career reflects a strong ability to integrate complex systems, communicate effectively across technical and operational teams, and mentor others toward success. My three overseas deployments—in Israel (2003) collaborating with the Israeli Army, and in Iraq (2005–2006, 2008–2009) supporting intelligence systems in combat environments—demonstrate leadership, cultural collaboration, and adaptability. Building on an Associate of Applied Science in Electrical Technology (2006), a Certificate in Fiber Optics (3M, 2005), and currently pursuing a Master’s Degree in Electrical Engineering (2023–Present, Expected 2028), I am committed to applying my military-honed skills to the fields of avionics, engineering, and technology. These deployments and responsibilities continue to shape my engineering career goals, reinforcing my commitment to leadership, adaptability, and cultural collaboration in technical fields.
